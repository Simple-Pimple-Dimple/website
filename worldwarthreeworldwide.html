World War Three Worldwide

World War Three Worldwide was a video game put into production by Darius Leong and Lucian Coveney in late 2019.

So, the player would pick a country, Australia for example, and then they'd look like a soldier from the country they'd picked.
They'd also have the same weapons as that country issues to their soldiers as well.
Darius and Lucian also thought of adding a creeper that follows you around and helps you (a tribute to Minecraft).

"I always thought of creepers being cute and sad.
I just wanted to help them, but of course they blew up before I had a chance." Explained Darius. "So we wanted to switch that around."

Darius wrote the game in JavaScript, the only language he knew at the time, and Lucian drew the artwork.

Sadly, Darius and Lucian pulled the plug on the project in early-ish 2020 because it was taking too long,
and because Darius found out that game developers should always start with an easy project.

They agreed to come back to the project after they'd established the business and gotten some good employees.
