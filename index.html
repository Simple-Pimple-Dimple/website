Who are we?

We are Worldwide Studios. Our company goal is to create games that are unique and different
If you are interested in the company, join our Discord Server


We started in late 2019, when Darius Leong had an idea for a video game
He contacted his friend Lucian Coveney, asking if Lucian could make the art
Lucian agreed, and they started development on World War Three Worldwide

Part way through development,
Darius and Lucian realised that they'd need a company to
manage the copyright between the two

And so, Worldwide Studios was created



All of our projects are open source so that people can know more about our programs



Where are we?

We are located in In Sydney,Australia in the Inner West
